Irena Sendler: An Unsung Hero

Photo of Irene Sendler with some children from the Holocaust.

Here are some things that Irena did during the Holocaust:

  • Worked for the Department of Social Welfare and Public Health of the City of Warsaw
  • Participated in war conspiratorial activities, such as rescuing Jews
  • Smuggled Jewish children out of the Warsaw Ghetto and then provided them with false identity documents and shelter
  • Arrested by the Gestapo but managed to hide the names of smuggled children despite torture
  • Sentenced to death but narrowly escaped the day before her execution
  • Credited with saving over 2500 children from the Holocaust

To read more about her heroic acts, check out her Wikipedia page.
